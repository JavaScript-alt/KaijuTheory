Character Profiles

King Kong

King Kong is a giant ape who battles various creatures and human forces in the MonsterVerse. Known for his strength and intelligence, Kong is a formidable protector of his territory.

Godzilla

Godzilla, the King of the Monsters, is a colossal reptilian Kaiju with immense power and radioactive breath. He is both a destroyer and a savior, maintaining balance in the world.

Scarking

Scarking is a newly introduced Kaiju known for its fierce battle tactics and dominance over the skies. With razor-sharp claws and wings, it is a force to be reckoned with.

Shimo

Shimo, the ice Kaiju, has the ability to control and manipulate ice. This cold-blooded creature is known for its strategic mind and icy demeanor in battles.
